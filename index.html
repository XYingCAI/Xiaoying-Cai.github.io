Yiming Chao

I love stem cell and data science ;)

I am a PhD student in stem cell biology from LKS Faculty of Medicine, the University of Hong Kong & Centre for Translational Stem Cell Biology.

Currently, I am working in Dr Rio Sugimura's lab using time-series single-cell RNA sequencing and 10x Visium spatial transcriptome to study early hematopoiesis in human EPSC-derived multi-lineage organoids. We aim to decipher the (extra)embryonic regulation on human hematopoiesis in our in vitro model. I am also working with Dr Yuanhua Huang on single-cell computational analysis of CRISPR/Cas9-based cellular lineage tracing system. We are establishing a new pipeline for dissecting the CRISPR barcodes and reconstruct the lineage tree with CARLIN (Cell, 2020) and KP-Tracer (Cell, 2022) datasets. We hope that our pipeline could be useful to all CRISPR-based molecular barcoding system.
